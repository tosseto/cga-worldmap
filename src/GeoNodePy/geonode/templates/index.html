WorldMap is an open source system for geospatial collaboration currently under construction. It is being built to assist academic research and teaching as well as the general public. The system will support data discovery, analysis, visualization, editing, commenting on, and archiving of multi-disciplinary, multi-source and multi-format data, organized spatially and temporally.

The first instance of WorldMap, focused on the continent of Africa, is called AfricaMap. Since its beta release in November of 2008, the framework has been implemented in several geographic locations with different research foci, including metro Boston, East Asia, Vermont, Harvard Forest and the city of Paris.
